## Unofficial State Election Results

<!-- image -->

The Board of Registrars will be meeting to open any vote-by-mail ballots postmarked by November 5, 2024 which need to be received by 5:00 p.m. on Friday November 8, 2024.  The meeting of the Board of Registrars must be held prior to November 15, 2024, after they have been processed, official results will become available and will be posted.

[Town of Cheshire UNOFFICIAL State Election Results 11-5-24](https://storage.googleapis.com/juniper-media-library/56/2024/11/State%20Election%2011-5-24%20Unofficial%20Results.pdf)

Questions should be directed to the Town Clerk at 413-743-1690 x. 104 or townclerk@cheshire-ma.gov
