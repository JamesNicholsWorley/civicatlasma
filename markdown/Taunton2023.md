CITY ELECTION 
NOVEMBER 07, 2023 
On November 07, 2023 the Taunton City General Election was held in sixteen precincts, two 
sub-precincts at fifteen pqlling locations throughout the City. The polls were opened at 7:00 AM and 
closed at 8:00 PM. There was Forty Thousand, nine hundred and Eighty Six Voters on record on 
Election Day. 
Nine Thousand, One Hundred and Fifty Two ballots were cast. Seven hundred and twenty 
six vote by mail ballots were mailed and six hundred and thirty six vote by mail ballots returned and 
cast. Ninety two absentee ballots were mailed and eighty six absentee ballots were returned and cast. 
Seven in-person absentee ballots were cast and seven hundred and ninety six in-person early voting 
ballots were cast. All ballots: Election Day and Alternative methods of voting were cast and 
tabulated on Election Day at their respective Ward and Precincts. 
The Election Wardens were lA-lAl Linda Nickerson, lB Carol Pimental, 2A Joan Kelley, 2B 
Sharon Weatherford, 3A Jean Smith, 3B Norma Barbour and Mabel Smith Co-Warden, 4A Deborah 
Tokarz -Cordeiro, 4B-4Bl Nicole Briggs, SA Shara Costa, SB Judith Corliss, 6A Lucinda Lynds, 6B 
Stasia Brackett, 7 A Dianne Cote, 7B Joseph McGowan, 8A Judy Benoit, and 8B Susan Davis. 
Mark L. Pacheco Election Director, Rosalind Grassie Assistant Director, Karen Andrade Head 
Administrative Clerk and Maria Dovale Head Clerk were also present. The City of Taunton Election 
Commissioners are Frank E. Biedak, Chairman, Jason C. Roomes, Carol A. Welch, and Melissa W. 
Langham.
