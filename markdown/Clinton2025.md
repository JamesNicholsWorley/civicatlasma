# Election Information

### State Primary Election September 1, 2026 7:00am - 8:00pm

Early Voting begins August 22 - 28 8:00am - 4:00pm

### State Election November 3, 2026 7:00am - 8:00pm

Early Voting Oct. 17 - 30 8:00am - 4:00pm

[Absentee Ballot Application](\DocumentCenter\View\1467)

To request an Absentee Ballot you must have an excuse as to why you cannot attend an election (see below.) While there is no excuse needed to vote early or Vote By Mail.

Absentee Ballot Requirements: Be away from our City/town on Election Day; or have a disability that keeps you from voting at your polling place; or have a religious belief that prevents you from voting at your polling place on Election Day.
