# 2024 Annual Town Election

The Town Election is normally held on the first or second Saturday in May with all precincts reporting to the Town Hall Auditorium from 8:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m.  Town Election for 2024 will be held on Saturday, May 4, 2024.
