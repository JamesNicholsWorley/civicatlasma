# Election Information Center &amp; Results

Clipart of a red checkmark and words stating Election Results

<!-- image -->

This page is for informational purposes only for all election-related materials and results. Please be advised that this is not the official posting place for any election warrants, sample ballot initiatives, or election press releases.  The Town Clerk's office will make every effort to post all materials on this page in a timely manner.  For any election-related questions, please do not hesitate to contact the Town Clerk's office a 508-669-5411, Option 2, or [email Shara Costa](mailto:scosta@dighton-ma.gov) .
