The polls for the Annual Town Election were opened at 9 a.m. and closed at 5 p.m.
1771 voters or 16% of the 11,705 registered voters cast ballots.  Included in this total were 76 absentee 
ballots and 622 early voting ballots.  Officers on duty were Lieutenant Christine May-Stafford.
The  unofficial results were announced by Town Clerk Dawn M. Varley at 
5:15 p.m.  The legal  meeting for the election of officers was held under a warrant issued by
Selectmen on 4/23/24 and posted on 4/29/24 in accordance with Town by-laws.
