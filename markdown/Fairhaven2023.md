Updated April 5, 2023, 9:40 a.m. ET

FAIRHAVEN - [Fairhaven](https://www.fairhaven-ma.gov/) voters hit the polls Monday resulting in change coming to the School Committee and Planning Board, with new names coming out on top of contested races for spots on both, according to unofficial, preliminary results provided on Tuesday.

In the race for two seats on the [**School Committee**](https://www.fairhaven-ma.gov/school-committee) , newcomer Stephanie Pickup topped the ballot with 803 votes, and incumbent Brian Monroe came in second with 780 votes.
