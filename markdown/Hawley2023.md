HAWLEY - Wayne Lemoine will take over the three-year assessor position, after receiving 24 write-in votes in Monday's election.

The race, against write-in candidate Ashley Harrison, was the only contested one. Harrison received 15 votes.

According to Town Clerk Donna Lemoine, 53 residents cast their ballots, making for a 22% voter turnout.

The results of the remaining races, which were all uncontested, are as follows:

■Selectboard/Board of Health, three-year term - Will Cosby, 41 votes.

■Town clerk, three-year term - Donna Lemoine, 50 votes.
