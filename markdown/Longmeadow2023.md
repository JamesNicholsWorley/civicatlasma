## TOWNOFLONGMEADOW ANNUALTOWNELECTIONMINUTES LONGMEADOWCOMMUNITYHOUSE June13,2023

The qualified voters of the Town met at the time and place appointed. The ballots were delivered to the polling place, the ballot boxes were shown to be empty, and the registers were set at zero to the satisfaction of the Election Officers and the Police Officers on duty. The polls were declared to be open at 7:00 a.m. Election Officers were as follows:

Wardens: Sandra MacFadyen, Patricia Self, Barbara Wenig

Clerks:  Chava Cohen, Ruth Griffiths, Mary Houser, David Russell, William Squires, Inspector/Checkers: Alan Barr, Elaine Barr, Ellie Bernstein, Ron Bernstein, Linda Betley, Mary Breeding, Donna Burrall, Steve Burrall, Patricia Capozza, Judith Civiok, Denise Conboy, Russ Desrosier, Cynthia Guertin, Philip Hallahan, Nancy Hayes, Ronald Johnston, Thomas Kucab, Kathy Lanouette, Linda Lasorsa, Vincent McPartland, Patricia Munson, Helene Murthy, Derek Paar, Aletha Prankus, Sheryl Reiter, Debbie Rickless, Carol Watts, Cathy Yurkunas

At 8:00 p.m. in the evening, the polls were declared closed. Tabulation was performed using optical scanning vote-counting equipment. Wardens and the Clerk began the tabulation procedure. When all precincts were tallied, the results were declared as follows:
