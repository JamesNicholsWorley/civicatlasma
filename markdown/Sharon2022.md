ANNUAL TOWN ELECTION 
May 17, 2022 
 
Pursuant to the provisions of the Warrant of April 12, 2022, the inhabitants of the Town of 
Sharon qualified to vote in elections, met in the Sharon High School Gymnasium at 7:00 A.M., 
Tuesday, May 17, 2022.  The meeting was called to order by Town Clerk Mark F. Hogan who 
read the call and return of the warrant.  The Warden of the election was Shelley Kahalas.  The 
Wardens for precincts 1, 2, 3, 4 and 5 were named as follows:  Karin Hagan, Susie Keating, 
Arlene Flatto, Susan Slater and Marion Baker.  Clerks and workers were:  Sadie Arundale, 
Trisha Brahmachari, Sally Brouhard, Marie Cuneo, Carol Dickerman, Judith Doo, India-Mae 
Fraser, Rahem Hamid, Nihar Iyengar,Ayaan Jibril, Jane Kinney, Kendra Kodira, Ivy Krull, Beth 
Lappen, Lauren Loomis, Zack Loomis, Marie Martin, Justin Meszler, Reeti Rawal, Susan Reed, 
Ernest Rotman, Shirley Schofield, Jeff Shapiro, Kaitlin Wiebe, and Mildred Worthley,  
 
The ballot boxes were shown to be empty and registered zero; the ballot boxes were locked and 
keys delivered to the Officer of the Day, Lieutenant Jeffrey D. Penders.  At 2:00pm the keys 
were transferred to the new Officer of the Day, Lieutenant Scott Leonard.  All election workers 
were sworn. 
 
At 8:00 P.M. the polls were declared closed. Total ballots tabulated at the polls (not including
