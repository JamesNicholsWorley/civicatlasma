**Warwick, MA Annual Town Election - May 20** **th** **, 2024**

There were 153 voters in total out of 681 total registered voters in Warwick. Thank you to our Constables, George Day &amp; Brian Peters; our Wardens, Johan Sednek &amp; Tracey Weed; our poll workers, Gloria Varno, Dawn Magi, David Koester, and Elaine Sednek; and our ballot counters, Barbara Walker, Jaylin Dibble, Dan Dibble, and Elaine Sednek.

Go ahead and mark your calendars for the next elections on September 3 rd &amp; November 5 th . Specifically, mark your calendars for 8pm, and come help count ballots - we're expecting a large turnout this fall, and we can literally never have too many people to counting!
