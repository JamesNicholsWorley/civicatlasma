# Election Night Report - 2025/11/04

<!-- image -->

Department of Innovation &amp; Technology

City of Worcester, MA

[Download](https://www.arcgis.com/sharing/rest/content/items/2c70038faab747c381f3a639bc876940/data)

## Summary

City of Worcester results from the final recount for the Municipal Election held on November 4, 2025.

The City of Worcester is comprised of five districts, ten wards and sixty plus precincts. The City of Worcester Election Office conducts all elections. Starting in 2022 the Election office offers election results in [an Excel-compatible format](https://opendata.worcesterma.gov/datasets/worcesterma::municipal-election-results-november-4-2025/about) , as well.

**More Information:** Visit the [Election Commission](https://www.worcesterma.gov/elections) webpage to learn more about their goals, programs, and initiatives.

[***Informing Worcester***](https://opendata.worcesterma.gov/) ***is the City of Worcester's open data portal where interested parties can obtain public information at no cost.***
