# Annual Town Election

The Ayer Annual Town Election will be held in the Great Hall at Ayer Town Hall on Tuesday, May 14, 2024, from 7 am to 8 pm.

Ayer Abs Proof Cropped-400x377

<!-- image -->

- [Election Results Official (PDF)](\DocumentCenter\View\465\Annual-Election---Offical-Election-Results-May-14-2024-PDF)
- [View the Specimen Ballot to enlarge (JPG)](\ImageRepository\Document?documentId=464) .
- The last day to register to vote in this election is Friday, May 3, at 5 pm.
- The last day to request (by mail)an absentee or vote by mail ballot is Tuesday, May 7, at 5 pm.
- The last day to request (in-person) absentee or vote by mail ballot is Monday, May 13, at noon.
- [Vote by Mail Application (PDF)](\DocumentCenter\View\467\2024-Vote-by-Mail-Application-PDF)
- The last day to request Nomination Papers was Friday, March 22, at noon
- The last day to return completed Nomination Papers was Tuesday, March 26, at 5 pm.
- Nomination papers are now available in the Town Clerk's office for those wishing to run for a Town Office. Nomination papers can be obtained during regular Town Hall business hours.
