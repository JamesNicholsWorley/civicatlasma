OFFICIAL ANNUAL TOWN ELECTION SAT, MAY 17, 2025
The polls for the Annual Town Election were opened at 9 a.m. and closed at 5 p.m. 2361 voters or 19.75%
of the 11,977 registered voters cast ballots. Included in this total were 161  absentee ballots and 30 EV ballots.
  The Officer on duty was Lieutenant Daniel Connolly.  The unofficial results were announced by Town Clerk
 Dawn M. Varley at 5:10 p.m.  The legal meeting for the election of officers was held under a
 warrant issued by Selectmen on 4/7/2025 and posted on 4/24/2025  in accordance with Town by-laws.
