# West Stockbridge municipal election unofficial results

According to town officials, only nine percent of registered voters in West Stockbridge voted in the municipal election.

Written by

[Shaw Israel Izikson](https://theberkshireedge.com/author/sizikson/)

May 12, 2025

<!-- image -->

**West Stockbridge - The following are the unofficial results of the municipal elections held on Monday, May 12, as provided by Town Administrator Marie Ryan:**

**Town Moderator** Joseph Roy Jr. 98 votes Write-ins three votes

**Select Board** Andrew Potter: 70 votes Write-ins: Six ballots

**Town Clerk** Veronica Barrett: 92 votes Blank ballots: 15

**Tax Collector** Kelli Bradbury: 99 votes Blank ballots: Eight

**Board of Assessors** Edward Sporn: 84 votes Write-ins: two ballots

**Finance Committee** Write-ins for Curt Wilson: 46 ballots Write-ins for Deb Bowen: 32 ballots Write-in ballot for other candidates: One ballot Blank ballots: 28

**Planning Board** Dana Bixby: 63 votes Write-ins: Nine ballots Blank ballots: 35

**Board of Health** Earl Moffatt: 83 votes Write-ins: Two ballots Blank ballots: 22

**Library Trustee** Molly Meador: 86 votes Write-ins: One ballot Blank ballots: 20

**Cemetery Commission** Marion Loring: 92 votes Blank Ballots: 15

**Ballot question: Town Clerk to Appointed Position** Yes: 54 No: 43 Blank ballots: 10

*Total voters for this election: 107 Total voters in town: 1171*
