[026 Town of Boylston Elected Officials](Documents\Government\Town Clerk\2026 Elected Officials.pdf?t=202605140915160)

See the Town Clerk's Webpage (blue Side Menu) for information on specific elections, primaries, and legal notices.

**Senior Citizen and Handicap Parking:** There  are handicap parking spaces available both at Town Hall and at Tahanto Regional High School.  On election and town meeting days we add temporary senior parking spaces.  If you need assistance at either location we are happy to help you - just reach out to the Town Clerk's Office, (508) 869-2234.

**Town of Boylston Polling Location:** Boylston's Election Day Polling Location is upstairs in the Hillside Gymnasium at Town Hall, 221 Main Street, Boylston.  Early Voting takes place on the lower level of Town Hall in the Selectmen's Chambers.  Town Meetings are held in the auditorium at Tahanto Regional High School, 1001`Main Street, Boylston.

**Polling Hours:** Polling hours are 12 p.m. to 8 p.m. for local elections.  Polling hours are 7 a.m. to 8 p.m. for state primaries and elections.  Town Meeting is held at 7 p.m.

**Voter Registration:** If you are new to town or moving within the town, we urge you to stop by the office to fill out a voter registration form.  You may also call (508) 869-2234 to request that one be mailed to you.  We are open Monday-Thursday, 8:00 a.m. to 2:00 p.m. and Monday evenings, 6:00 p.m.-8:00 p.m.

To register online please visit the

[Online Voter Registration System through the Secretary of the Commonwealth of Massachusetts.](https://www.sec.state.ma.us/OVR/)
