## WARNING: Crystal Lake and Bakers Pond

The Orleans Health &amp; Human Services Department has issued a warning after a potential toxic algal bloom was identified at Crystal Lake and Bakers Pond. Swimming and water contact are not recommended for people or pets. The previous potential toxic algal bloom warning at Uncle Harvey's Pond has been lifted. Anyone who may have been exposed to the water should rinse off as soon as possible. The Health Department is monitoring the situation and will share updates as they become available.
