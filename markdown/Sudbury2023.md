# [Town Elections - March 27, 2023 ( Warrant ) | Back to List](https://sudbury.ma.us/clerk/?p=3918/)

The Annual Town Election was held at two locations. Precincts 1, 2, 3 &amp; 5 voted at the Fairbank Community Center, 40 Fairbank Road, and Precincts 4 &amp; 6 voted at the the Town Hall, 322 Concord Road. The polls were open from 7:00 AM to 8:00 PM. There were 3353 votes cast, representing 25.57% of the town's 13,112 registered voters. The final tabulation was done at the Town Hall.
