# Town Clerk Office

## [Please call (413) 528-4536 extension 102 for an appointment or email the Clerk with any questions.](tel:(413) 528-4536)

Town Meetings and Town Elections are held in the Alford Town Hall. The Town Offices are located in the former Alford School House.

- Annual Town Meetings are held on the 2nd Tuesday in May at 6:00pm.
- Town Elections are held on the 3rd Tuesday in May from 12:00pm to 6:00pm.
- All Special Town Meetings and Public Hearings are called by Board of Selectmen or the appropriate Board Chairman. All meetings are posted according to the Massachusetts Open Meeting Laws.
- The Official Town Notices are posted at the following locations:
    - Transfer Station
    - Crooked Hill Road &amp; Green River Valley Road (Route 71)
    - Town Offices
