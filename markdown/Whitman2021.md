The polls for the Annual Town Election were opened at 9 a.m. and closed at 5 p.m.
914 voters or 9% of the 10,900 registered voters cast ballots.  Included in this total were 32 absentee 
ballots and 177 early voting ballots.  Officers on duty were Lieutenant Christine May-Stafford and 
Lieutenant Daniel Connolly.  The  unofficial results were announced by Town Clerk Dawn M. Varley at 
5:08 p.m.  The legal  meeting for the election of officers was held under a warrant issued by Selectmen 
on 4/20/21 and posted on 4/29/21 in accordance with Town by-laws.
