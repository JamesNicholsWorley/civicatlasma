Report of the Presidential Primary March 5th,2024

In accordance with the warrant, the inhabitants of Brewster, qualified to vote in the Presidential Primary met at the Brewster Baptist Church, 1848 Main Street, Brewster, on Tuesday, March 5th, 2024.

The polls were declared open at 7:00 am.

The elections workers duly sworn-in were:Charles Winn,Warden; Joan Scheffer and Glenda Normand, Precinct 1 Clerk; June Cameron, Precinct 2 Clerk; Lauren Elliot-Grunes and Sharon Ryone, Precinct 3 Clerk; Checkers and Counters were as follows; Cheryl Avery, Anne Best, Dave Briggs, Nancy Burdych, Susan Daly, Jim Eddy, Mary Eddy, Beth Finch, Robert Harris, Amy Harbeck, Rheanna Hastings, Peter Johnson, Sandra Johnson, Judy Lagergren, James Lieb, John Martens, Cynthia Mathison, Julie Menges, Mary Jo Nabywaniec, Marjorie Oliver, Marcia Otto, Peter Scheffer, Bruce Semple, Patricia Stanley, Elizabeth Taylor, Thomas Thatcher, and Jayanne Monger, Assistant Town Clerk.
