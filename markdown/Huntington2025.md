## 5/11/2024 Annual Municipal Election Final Results

The Annual Town Election was held on May 11, 2024. A total of 98 votes were cast for 14 positions and one ballot question.

In the only contest on the ballot, John McVeigh won the 3-year Board of Selectmen position with 47 votes over challenger Anthony J. Loughran's 42 votes.

For Regional School Committee 3-years, Lisa Goding ran unopposed winning with 85 votes. Other unopposed positions on the ballot include: George Peterson III for Board of Health 3-years with 84 votes and Moderator with 79 votes, Christine Stochlinski for Board of Assessors 3-years with 83 votes, Michelle Fieldstad-Booth for Town Clerk 3-years with 87 votes, H. Daniel Oliveira for Water &amp; Sewer Commissioner 3-years with 86 votes, Charles Dazelle for Constable 3-years with 83 votes, Karen Wittshirk for Trustee, Alphonso Pettis Fund 3-years with 84 votes and Trustee, Library 3-years with 86 votes.

Several positions on the ballot were filled in by Write in candidates, Deresa Helems for Constable 2-years with 6 votes, Henry Sarafin for Tree Warden 1-year with 2 votes. Two other positions that failed to elect a candidate were Regional School District 2-year and Trustee, Whiting Street Fund 3-years.

The ballot question pertaining to changing the Town Clerk position from elected to appointed passed with 52 yes, 37 no and 9 blanks.

## **NOTICE FROM THE TOWN CLERK**

Nomination Papers for the upcoming Town Election on Saturday, May 11, 2024 are available at the Town Clerk's office during regular business hours.  The following list of offices is available for Nomination Papers:

2024:

Board of Selectmen (3 yr)

Town Clerk (3 yr)

Board of Health (3 yr)

Board of Assessors (3 yr)

Water &amp; Sewer Commission (3 yr)

Regional School Committee (3 yr)

Regional School Committee (2 yr)

Tree Warden (1 yr)

Moderator (1 yr)

Trustees, Alphonso P. Pettis Fund (3 yr)

Constable (3 yr)

Constable (2 yr)

Trustees, Huntington Library (3 yr)

Trustees, Whiting Street Fund (3 yr)

**Last day to obtain Nomination Papers** is **Wednesday, March 6, 2024 .**

**All Nomination Papers drawn must be** submitted to the Registrars of Voters via Town Clerk **by Wednesday, March 20, 2024 at 5:00 pm.**

The **last date/time to withdraw or object** with Clerk's Office is **Friday, April 5** , **2024** at 5:00 pm.

Potential nominees **must be registered voters in Huntington for 9 months as of January 1** and must obtain a **minimum** of 20 **valid** signatures of **registered** Huntington voters (we recommend 25+ in case any are invalid due to illegible signatures, addresses, etc).  Please make sure that all signatures and addresses are legible (they may also be printed on same line), as they will be rejected if they are not.

**The last day to register to vote in this election will be on Wednesday, May 1, 2024 at the Town Clerk's office during the hours of 3:00 - 5:00 p.m. and 6:00 - 8:00 p.m.**

**Early voting will be held in the Town Clerk's office on Wednesday, May 1, 2024 during the hours of 3:00 - 5:00 p.m. and 6:00 - 8:00 p.m. and on Monday, May 6, 2024 during the hours of 6:00 - 8:00 p.m.**

Several of the listed posts do not have incumbents running for reelection, so need candidates to avoid vacancies.  Please email [townclerk@huntingtonma.us](mailto:townclerk@huntingtonma.us) with any questions at your earliest convenience.

Thank you!
