GILL - There was a "light turnout" of 70 voters who cast ballots during Monday' annual town election where all positions were uncontested, according to Town Clerk Doreen Stevens.

Winners are as follows:

■Selectboard/Sewer Commission, three-year term - Gregory Snedeker, incumbent, 68 votes.

■Assessor, three-year term - Pam Lester, incumbent, 65 votes.

■Cemetery Commission, three-year term - Patricia Hawkins, 68 votes.

■Tax collector, three-year term - Thomas Hodak, incumbent, 62 votes.
