# 2025 Annual Town Election

Event Date:

Thursday, May 15, 2025 - 12:00pm to 8:00pm

Aquinnah Annual Town Election May 15th, 2025 Aquinnah Town Hall - Noon to 8:00pm

Important Annual Town Election Information: Applying for and Returning Mailed Ballots EARLY Vote By Mail (VBM) IS AVAILABLE TO ALL REGISTERED VOTERS for the Annual Town Election. To access a mailed ballot application please go HERE. You may mail, email or drop off the application at Town Hall to the Clerk Dept. Applications are also available at the Town Hall. Mail to: Aquinnah Town Clerk, 955 State Road, Aquinnah, MA 02535 Email a scan of the application to: - [townclerk@aquinnah-ma.gov](mailto:townclerk@aquinnah-ma.gov) Drop off application at: 955 State Road, Aquinnah, MA 02535

Please go HERE to apply to Vote By Mail through an ONLINE module. This online application does not allow a voter to choose just the local election on May 15th, 2025. In order to use the online application and receive a mailed ballot for the town election you must select ALL ELECTIONS. The deadline for receipt of a VBM or mailed Absentee Ballot application, to apply to be mailed an Annual Town Election ballot, is May 8th, 2025, 5PM. You may write a signed letter to your local election office to apply for your mailed ballot and send via email or regular mail or drop off at Town Hall. VBM applications may be submitted by mail (955 State Road, Aquinnah, MA 02535), email ( [townclerk@aquinnah-ma.gov](mailto:townclerk@aquinnah-ma.gov) ), in-person or into the Town Clerk drop box located in the front entryway of the town office on the wall.

Mailed Ballot applications that are submitted are processed promptly and the ballot is usually mailed by the end of the next business day. Ballots must be returned by 8:00PM on May 15th, 2025 to be counted:

By mail: Aquinnah Town Clerk 955 State Road, Aquinnah, MA 02535 using the enclosed white mailing envelope that is pre-addressed and prepaid Into the Dropboxes or In Person: Aquinnah Town Offices 955 State Road, Aquinnah, MA 02535 Call the Aquinnah Town Clerk for more information 508-645-2304 Last day to register to vote at the Annual Town Meeting and the Annual Town Election is May 5th, 2025, ten day before the town meeting.
