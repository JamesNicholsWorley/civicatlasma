OFFICIAL ANNUAL TOWN ELECTION SAT, MAY 21, 2022
The polls for the Annual Town Election were opened at 9 a.m. and closed at 5 p.m. 1308 voters or 11.78%
of the 11,114 registered voters cast ballots. Included in this total were 150  absentee ballots.  The Officer
on duty was Lieutenant Daniel Connolly.  The unofficial results were announced by Town Clerk
 Dawn M. Varley at 5:10 p.m.  The legal meeting for the election of officers was held under a
 warrant issued by Selectmen on 4/15/2022 and posted on 4/25/2022  in accordance with Town by-laws.
