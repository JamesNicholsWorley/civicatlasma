# Election results: Winner victorious in Bolton Select Board race

[Jan Gottesman](\staff\5937240002\jan-gottesman) The Item May 12, 2022, 2:23 a.m. ET Keep Watching

Emily Winner

<!-- image -->

BOLTON - Emily Winner easily defeated Alan DiPietri (601-46) for a three-seat on the Bolton Board of Selectmen in Monday's voting. Neither previously have held elected office.

Jonathan Keep chose not to run for re-election for his seat.

There were no other contested seats.

Winner had not responded to a request for a comment before The Item deadline.

Others elected Monday were:

• Moderator, one year, incumbent Douglas Storey, 576 votes;

• Town Clerk, one year, incumbent Pamela Powell, 596;

• Board of Assessors, three years, incumbent Wendy Rogers, 576;

• Cemetery Committee, three years, incumbent Betsy Taylor-Kennedy, 569;

• Constables, five seats, three years each, incumbents Barbara Brown (548), Harold Brown (530), David Goulding (542), and Sean Collins (525); and Christopher Rogers (530).

• Board of Health, three years, Wendy Karlon, 555;

• Library Trustee, three years, Caroline Robertson, 557;

• Nashoba Regional School District Committee, three years, Amy Vessels, 538;

• Parks and Recreation, two seats, three years each, incumbent Edward Sterling, 490, and Erika Mond, 489;

• Planning Board, five years, incumbent Michael Gorr, 549; four year seat, James Barr, 536.

There were 655 votes, a 16% turnout of the 4,223 registered voters.
