From left, Littleton Select Board member Matthew Nordhaus, Planning Board member Anna Hueston and newly elected Select Board member Karen Morrison stand in front of their campaign tables on election day at the Littleton Middle School, May 6, 2023. LITTLETON - Incumbent Matthew Nordhaus and newcomer Karen Morrison earned the two open seats on the Select Board in Saturday's election, beating two-term incumbent Cindy Napoli.

Morrison, a former attorney who consults with small businesses, led the race by 1,202 votes to Nordhaus' 1,054, according to results [provided](https://www.littletonma.org/sites/g/files/vyhlif8011/f/pages/recap_-official_ballottotal_registered_voters_733722.pdf) by the Town Clerk's Office. Napoli received 627 votes.
