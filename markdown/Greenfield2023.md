## 

Greenfield - a great place to live, work, and play!

## 

Enjoy the Green River Festival and many other exciting events in Greenfield!

## 

Greenfield is home to a wide array of businesses ranging from eclectic shops to tech startups. Don't forget - you help your neighbors when you shop local!

## 

The City of Greenfield has a mayoral form of government with a mayor and city council.

## 

The staff of the City of Greenfield is ready to help you!
