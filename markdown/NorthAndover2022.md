NORTH ANDOVER, MA - North Andover voters elected a new member to its school committee Tuesday night.

Joseph Hicks defeated Kevin Dube and get the open seat on the school committee, according to unofficial election results released by the Town Clerk's Office.

Hicks finished with 1,444 votes, while Dube got the support of 823 North Andover residents.
