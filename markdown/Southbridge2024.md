# News Flash

## Retirement Board

Posted on: December 19, 2024

### [ARCHIVED] Election Results

**ANNOUNCEMENT OF ELECTION RESULTS**

December 19, 2024

Please be advised that as a result of the election held in the Town Hall Retirement Office, Southbridge MA on December 19, 2024, Wilfrid Cournoyer was elected as the Third Member of the Southbridge Retirement Board for a term which will commence on January 1, 2025 and expire on December 31, 2027.

**Wil Cournoyer, Incumbent - 108 votes**

**Gary Fontaine - 76 votes**

Yolanda Alvarado

Election Officer/Retirement Administrator
